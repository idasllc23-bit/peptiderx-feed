Privacy Policy — Peptide RX

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Peptide RX (“the app,” “we,” “us”) is an educational research-reference app about peptides. Plain version: we don’t collect your personal information.

No account, no sign-in

Peptide RX does not require an account. We do not ask for your name, email, password, or any login. There is no user profile stored on our servers because we do not operate a user-data server.

Information stored on your device

The app saves your choices only on your device (using the device’s local storage): the theme you pick, your onboarding answers (the goals/symptoms you select or type) used to show you relevant research locally, and the U.S. state you select on the “Connect with a Doctor” screen. This information never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us. You can erase it at any time by deleting the app.

Information we receive

We do not receive your personal information. The app does not include analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers.

Network requests the app makes

To stay current, the app downloads a single public data file (peptide information, news, podcast links, and the clinic directory) from a public URL. This is a normal file download — the request contains no personal information about you, and we do not log who downloads it. The app also opens links you tap (research sites, podcast pages, clinic websites, phone/email links) in your device’s browser, podcast app, phone dialer, or mail app. Once you leave Peptide RX, the privacy policy of that third party applies.

Children

Peptide RX is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Medical disclaimer

Peptide RX provides general research information only. It is not medical advice and is not a substitute for consultation with a licensed healthcare professional. Always talk to your doctor before making any decision about peptides.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above.
