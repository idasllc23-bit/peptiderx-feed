How PeptideRx works

PeptideRx is a telemedicine platform. We work exclusively with licensed compounding pharmacies, and every medication is dispensed only after a valid prescription from a licensed U.S. physician.

1

Connect with a licensed physician

Patients find a clinic in their state and consult a licensed U.S. physician about whether peptide therapy is appropriate for them.

2

Receive a valid prescription

If treatment is clinically appropriate, the physician issues a valid prescription. No medication is provided without one.

3

Filled by a licensed pharmacy

The prescribed medication is prepared by a state-licensed compounding pharmacy and shipped directly to the patient.

All medications require a valid prescription from a licensed U.S. physician. PeptideRx provides software and platform services and facilitates prescription fulfillment by independent licensed pharmacies; it does not practice medicine, dispense medications, or provide medical advice.

Please note: PeptideRx is a telemedicine platform that connects patients with independent, licensed U.S. physicians and licensed compounding pharmacies. It does not provide medical advice, does not practice medicine, does not dispense medications, and does not guarantee treatment.
